The Dynamics of Investment, Payout and Debt

Posted on 2022-08-22 Edited on 2022-09-17

Content

  1. Develop a dynamic agency model of a public corperation in which the interactions of investment debt and payout decisions can change drastically depending on manegers’ preferences
  2. Set the model of CAPEX(payout and managerial rents plus investment), borrowing, payout and rents
  3. Start by valuing the firm if it were run by risk-neutral shareholders, then introduce risk-averse managers
  4. the dynamic agency models assume risk-neutral managers, while individual investors’ consumption-saving desitions and portfolio choices assume risk aversion. We must leave the specification of managers’ preferences as an important topic for investigation

Points

  1. CARA and CRRA utility
  2. Euler equation
  3. closed form solution
