Optimal decision-making of consumption, investment and annuity purchasing in retirement plan

Posted on 2022-08-09 Edited on 2022-08-12

Content

  1. Indicate the significance of retirement plans in the background of population aging
  2. Analyze the theoretical model of annuities, zero coupon bond, and individual wealth and estimate the parameters
  3. Figure out the optimal portfolio choice for various retirees and in different market environments
  4. Do numerical analysis and verify the effect of deferred annuities on the consumption and utility level of retirees

Models

  1. Vasicek interest rate model(OU process)
  2. Gompert mortality model(exponential distribution)
  3. Annuity factor
  4. Epstein-Zin utility model(distinguish relative risk aversion(RRA) and elasticity of intertemporal substitution(EIS) effectively)

Numerical Algorithm

recurse backward, dimension reduction, Nelder-Mead method, Gauss-Legendre quadrature, cubic spline interpolation, Kalman Filtering
